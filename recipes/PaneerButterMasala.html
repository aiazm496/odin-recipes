Description:

Paneer butter masala, as the name contains the most used ingredient in vegetarian recipes. It is very delicious and is served in every restaurant which serves vegetarian food.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Paneer
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Masalas
  • Salt

Steps

  • Add oil and onion.
  • Let it turn brown.
  • Add tomato and salt.
  • Once the tomato becomes like thick paste, add paneer.
  • Let it cook for 10minutes.
